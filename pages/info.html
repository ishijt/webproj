Info

SchoolQuizZone is an educational website designed to make learning enjoyable through interactive games. It features multiple games across five subjects: Geography, Mathematics, Music, Biology, and English. The website is a collaborative project developed for Oamk’s Web Project course.

The homepage, info page, and score tracking page were created collectively by the project team. The scores page shows the total points earned and detailed results for each game. Each game page was independently designed and implemented by team members:

Geography: Flags and Capitals

Developed by Irina, the Geography section invites players to explore the world through two exciting games:

- Flags: Identify national flags in a 10-question multiple-choice quiz.

- Capitals: Match countries with their capitals in another 10-question challenge.

Music:

Developed by Ilari. The "Music Game" has three modes of questions: Sound clip mode, Image mode, and Trivia mode.

Each mode has a different type of answer style: Four choices, text input, and True/False answers. Music Game is 10 questions long.

Biology:

Developed by Heini. The "Name the animal" game challenges players to test their animal knowledge by indentifying animals from pictures. Players will see ten animal images and must correctly identify each one to score points. With a total of ten pictures, it's possible to earn up to ten points.

English:

Developed by Daniel. Try to guess the word that the image represents. You’ll see the correct word as a clue, but the letters are scrambled! Game challenges to improve players vocabulary.

Mathematics:

Developed by Erkka. Watch as multiplication problems fall from the sky like raindrops. Type the correct answers before they hit the bottom to score points.
